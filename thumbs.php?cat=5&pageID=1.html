My philosophy is that I can make anyone look good! I will begin by asking you exactly what type of portrait you are looking to achieve, as portraiture is such a broad genre. For instance, you may want some fun family portraits, shot against a bright white background, or you might be after a moody black and white shot in subdued light. For business or website portraits I will ask you about the message you want your portrait to convey to your viewers. I can do formal or informal, all for a very reasonable price, and if you wish, can put you in touch with lots of satisfied past customers.

I specialise in child, baby and family portraits. I am the mother of two daughters and have lots of experience with babies and children. For a time I worked as a nursery photographer, but gave up the job because of the rigid restrictions set by the company. I am frequently asked to do portraits for professional publications, graduation portraits and passport photographs (any nationality), but I am not interested in photographing weddings. I can, however, recommend some of my former students who specialise in this area. I am a one-woman business, and as such  the service I offer is personal and flexible. I do not do package deals, but will negotiate on price for larger orders.

Whatever your requirements or preferences, please telephone or email to discuss them with me.
