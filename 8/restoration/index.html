I take a great interest in old photographs and have all the equipment and skills to either reproduce them, enlarge and enhance them or restore them if they are damaged. Most of the work is done digitally although new wet prints can be made if the negatives are still in your possession. Restoration work is slow and painstaking - often, I think like plastic surgery - but the results are really worth the effort and cost if you have a special photograph. Please contact me to discuss any old photos you might want reproduced or restored.
