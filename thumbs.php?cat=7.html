I am a trained teacher and have been teaching Adult Education classes in Photography since 2001. Many satisfied students can testify to the efficacy of my teaching - several have turned professional. Classes are run by Abingdon and Witney Collge ( formerly Oxfordshire Adult Education) and privately.

Please See the Oxford Courses section of my website for further details of courses in Oxfordshire.

For further information about any of these courses, please use the Contact page, or to enrol, telephone 01235 555585 or visit www.abingdon-witney.ac.uk. Select 'Part time courses' and then 'Photography'. My classes are the ones located in Kidlington.

Private Classes: I teach various private classes from my home in East Oxford, also one to one tuitiion. For further information please contact me through the Contact page. Unfortunately I am unable to put my mobile number on this website as I receive a lot of unwanted and spam calls, but if you email me, I can give you my number.

Courses in the Isle of Wight: I am planning to run residential short courses from our rural retreat in the Isle of Wight. Please contact me if you would be interested in taking part as I am building up a database of interested parties, or if you would like to rent the annex (sleeps 4 including one double) for a weekend.
